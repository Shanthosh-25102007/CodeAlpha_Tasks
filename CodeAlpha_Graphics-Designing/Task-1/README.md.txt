# Adidas Logo Redesign

This project was completed as part of a Graphics Designing internship task.

## Project Overview

The objective of this task was to redesign any brand logo while preserving its recognizable identity and introducing a modern visual style.

## Design Concept

The redesigned adidas logo retains the iconic three-stripe structure of Adidas, representing growth, performance, and progress.

A glitch-inspired effect with red and blue accents was added to create a dynamic and futuristic appearance. This effect symbolizes:

* Innovation and modern technology
* Energy and movement
* Adaptability in the digital era
* A bold and youthful brand image

The design aims to appeal to modern audiences while maintaining the strong recognition of the original Adidas brand.
